About Me

Hey there! I'm Jessica, a second-year Computer Engineering major at UT Austin. I started my journey into coding as a 7th grader taking an intro to HTML class, which is probably why I'm primarily interested in front-end development. When I'm not stressing over which color scheme to use for this website, I love exciting students about entrepreneurship with 3 Day Startup Austin as a recruitment lead and attending workshops with the Design Collective and Mobile App Development.

Along with trying to become Pinterest famous, one of my favorite things to do is help people be successful through my blogs-turned-jobs. I'm currently helping young women succeed in college at JessicaSlaughter.co and teaching people how to start their own blog at TheBloggingBrew.com (because one just wasn't enough).
